# Retention Strategy Recommendations

## Champion

### Characteristics
- High spend
- High purchase frequency
- Strong engagement

### Strategy
- VIP loyalty rewards
- Early product access
- Exclusive offers

### Budget Priority
HIGH

---

## Loyal Customer

### Characteristics
- Consistent purchasing behavior
- Strong monetary value

### Strategy
- Personalized product recommendations
- Upsell and cross-sell campaigns

### Budget Priority
MEDIUM-HIGH

---

## Potential Loyalist

### Characteristics
- Good engagement
- Moderate spending

### Strategy
- Nurture campaigns
- Loyalty-program enrollment
- Repeat-purchase incentives

### Budget Priority
MEDIUM

---

## At Risk

### Characteristics
- Reduced activity
- Declining purchase behavior

### Strategy
- Immediate retention campaigns
- Personalized outreach
- Limited-time offers

### Budget Priority
HIGH

---

## Dormant

### Characteristics
- Long inactivity
- Low engagement

### Strategy
- Win-back campaigns
- Re-activation offers
- Feedback surveys

### Budget Priority
LOW-MEDIUM


# Budget Prioritization

| Segment | Priority | Reason |
|----------|----------|----------|
| Champion | High | Highest customer lifetime value |
| At Risk | High | Immediate churn prevention opportunity |
| Loyal Customer | Medium-High | Can be converted into Champions |
| Potential Loyalist | Medium | Growth opportunity |
| Dormant | Low-Medium | Lower recovery probability |
